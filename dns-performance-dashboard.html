How to Change DNS on Windows 11

After finding the fastest DNS provider, follow these steps to apply the settings.

  1. Open Settings, go to Network & internet, then select your connection type (e.g., Wi-Fi or Ethernet).
  2. Click on Hardware properties.
  3. Next to the "DNS server assignment" label, click the Edit button.
  4. In the dialog box, select Manual from the dropdown menu.
  5. Turn on the switch for IPv4.
  6. In the "Preferred DNS" and "Alternate DNS" boxes, enter the IP addresses you copied from your chosen provider above.
  7. Click Save and the new settings will be applied. Your web browsing should now be faster and more responsive.
